About me

I am a recent PhD graduate from the Department of Statistical Science at Duke University, where I was advised by Peter Hoff.
Prior to coming to Duke, I received my bachelor's degree from the University of Toronto and had the opportunity to collaborate with Nancy Reid there.

I am excited about what the future holds in store. This year I will be working with Mathias Drton as a Postdoctoral researcher in Munich.
In the summer of 2024, I will be joining the Department of Mathematical and Statistical Sciences at the University of Alberta as an assistant professor.
